General Information

Only 10 Recordings May Be Recorded At A Time

All Unsaved Recordings Will Be Deleted Upon Exiting The Current Web Session

In Order To Record More Than 10 Paw Scripts In One Web Session, You Will Need To Delete Them From The Queue On The Play Tab

0-9 On The Keyboard Correspond To The Recordings Displayed On The Play Tab With 0 Being The Top Recording



Keyboard Shortcuts

ESC - Toggles The Record Function

0-9 - Plays The Corresponding Loaded Recording

Shift + 0-9 - Downloads The Corresponding Loaded Recording
